Kerith World School

Meeting you at where ever you are

Kerith World School, K-12 Coaching & Schooling, is complete education system for students from Pre-School- LKG, Primary, Upper Preprimary, Secondary School and Senior Secondary level, is designed for students from 3.5 to 17 years of age with a need of interactive virtual Schooling and Special Coaching for Medical/ IIT-JEE/Civil Service Foundations

Online Schooling- Tuitions Class Pre-School-12

Kerith World School, Online Platform with easily accessible and interactive, live Classes, video tutorials, e-Content, materials enable students to establish a personal learning style. Therefore, our students can process course materials gradually and acquire new knowledge faster and easier. Students could learn from the comfort of their homes at online schooling,

At online schooling, each student creates a timetable for themselves. They are directly responsible for how long they learn and whether it is enough. In case they are struggling with a certain subject, they can always schedule live teaching support and could master lessons with the help of our teachers

Our Features

Online Study Material

E-Content Series starts from Pre-School to 12th Standard/Grade level which is a Student friendly e-Content Offline Software with 2D animations, Live Notes and Videos, Chapter Wise Notes. There will be Multiple Choice/Descriptive type Question Bank and Answers at the end of each Chapter which are prepared as per CBSE, ICSE and various State syllabuses in India. Since it is an offline software, the Students isn’t required to be online all the time. In fact, in some cases, a teacher may not even be necessary, as all of the lessons are designed to be completed without a tutor on-hand. It means, there may be detailed instructions and questions at the end of each lesson to scale student’s progress and performance. Students are able to review a core piece of content within the lesson for a longer period of time, in order to boost their retention of the information.

Teacher Support

Teachers are here to monitor and support the progress of individual students and help them to improve the areas where they have most difficulties. They evaluate students memory performance and self-organization skills along with their individual perceptive power of learning.

In case a student needs an additional support, we are organizing one on one teaching support. A teacher will schedule a tutoring session with a student and work together. A student can ask for teaching support on a one-time basis or in continuity. Our teachers are available to students at all time incase a student comes across an information that requires further explanation. 

Parent Support

Parents of our current or future students can always be in touch with our Education Counsellors to discuss any aspect of Coaching & Schooling at Kerith World School, and can access our learning platform to follow the progress of the student. Kerith World School Teachers and Counsellors will review students’ progress periodically and make you aware about their present academic status. You can request a meeting, simply by sending us an email at kerithworldschool@kerith.in or a call at 9388230001 , 9388230002 and we consider it as at the earliest.. 

Entrance Coaching

Class XI-XII
Medical, IIT-JEE, Civil Service
TWO YEAR INTEGRATED ENTRANCE COACHING PROGRAM
CENTERS: CHALAKUDY, KUTTIKKADU, MAVELIKKARA

Students (Medical / Engineering Aspirants) who are seeking for Admissions for XI Academic Year 2020-2021 and interested in an extensive Entrance Coaching can apply. All students who pass the 10th Exam are eligible for applying for admission to TWO YEAR INTEGRATED ENTRANCE COACHING PROGRAM. Students who are focusing on NEET, KEAM,IIT-JEE and other Medical/ Engineering Entrance Exam can join the Program. This program will cover first year Portions in the First Year Itself and Second Year Topics will be Finished by end of December (Digital Learning Series/Class Room Series). Students will have about 100 Examinations throughout Two Years Apart from E-content Series. Digital Learning Series (Online Entrance Class ) will be initiated from the month of August 2020 and Students can join the Class Room Series after Lockdown/ Restrictions. Student is free to clear doubts by way of interaction with faculty or send their question to the Academic Wing and it will be answered. E-Learning Series will be given to all students as their Study Materials and can be used in Desk Top/ Lap Top computers. Exams will be conducted as online and result can be viewed in online itself. We offer meritorious Scholarships to the Top Scorers XI and XII from regular Entrance Students. There will be a regular time table during our TWO YEAR PROGRAM Which includes study time, online classes, mock tests, relaxation time, recreations, prayer, play and exercise etc. Students who are join the program is not required for any extra tuitions for PCMB Subjects. Classes for CIVIL SERVICE Examinations are also conducted by KERITH to help Civil Service aspirants to achieve their goal.(interested can join the program by remitting extra fees)
